Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic disease that occurs when the body is unable to regulate blood sugar levels effectively. It can lead to serious health complications such as heart disease, kidney failure, and nerve damage if left untreated.

Learn More

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Hypertension is a condition where the force of blood against the artery walls is too high, increasing the risk of heart disease and stroke. It often has no symptoms but can be managed with lifestyle changes and medication.

Learn More

Asthma

Asthma is a condition in which your airways narrow and swell, leading to difficulty breathing. It often triggers coughing, wheezing, and shortness of breath. Proper treatment can help keep it under control.

Learn More

Heart Disease

Heart disease is a broad term for conditions that affect the heart, such as coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, and congenital heart defects. Leading a heart-healthy lifestyle is the best way to prevent it.
